Hi! Welcome to my personal website!

My name is John Redfern, and I am currently a junior in the BSI Program following the UX Design pathway. I am originally from Lake Tahoe, Nevada, and I found out about Michigan because my neighbors used to be professors here. I was the first student from my high school to ever come to college out here. In high school, I played football, baseball, golf, and basketball but my favorite was basketball, and I still enjoy playing pickup games with my friends or anyone who is at the gym! Oh, and I love to ski!

In my free time, I enjoy any and all outdoor activities. Growing up in Lake Tahoe gave me a great appreciation for the beauty of nature, and it gave me a love for all things outdoors. Watersports and winter sports are my absolute favorite, and if I had the opportunity to move to and live in the Swiss Alps skiing all day, I'd probably take it!

I've worked multiple different random jobs in my life. In Tahoe, there are many seasonal job positions only available for a part of the year. I worked as a golf course maintenance worker during the summer and a ski lift operator in the winter. My favorite job was working at a marina as a dockhand. I enjoyed it because a lot of the job involved interacting with people, and I am a social person. Additionally, the marina was home to many very, very nice and expensive Formula boats. I learned a pretty useful life skill in the ability to skillfully maneuver boats which I could use if I ever wanted to obtain a captain's license in the future.
